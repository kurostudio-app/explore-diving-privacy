# Privacy Policy for Explore Diving
**Last updated: November 28, 2025**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Explore Diving** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") handles your information.

**In Short:** We prioritize an "offline-first" design. We do not collect, store, or transfer your Personal Identifiable Information (PII) to our own servers.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

### 2.1 Personal Data
We do not knowingly collect any personal data. The App does not require registration or login. All your preferences (such as "Favorites" or search history) are stored locally on your device storage and are not transmitted to us.

### 2.2 Location Information
The App does not track your real-time location in the background. 
* **Map Display:** Location data may be processed locally on your device to show your position relative to dive sites on the map.
* **Navigation:** When you use the "Navigate" feature, the App passes the coordinates of the dive site to your external map application (e.g., Google Maps or Apple Maps). We do not store this interaction.

## 3. Third-Party Services
To provide app functionality, we use the following third-party services. These services may collect information (such as IP addresses) consistent with their own privacy policies.

* **Map Tiles (OpenStreetMap & CartoDB):** We use OpenStreetMap data rendered by CartoDB to display map tiles. When your device loads these map images, your request (including IP address and User Agent) is sent directly to the tile servers to retrieve the data. This is a standard internet protocol function.
    * [OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy](https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy)
    * [Carto Privacy Policy](https://carto.com/privacy/)

* **External Navigation (Google Maps / Apple Maps):**
    Links to navigation services are handled by your device's operating system. Your interaction with these external apps is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## 4. Advertising
Currently, this App does not include any external advertising SDKs (e.g., AdMob) and does not track user behavior for advertising purposes.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our local records (if any existed).

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us

# 隱私權政策 (Explore Diving 探索潛水)
**最後更新日期：2025年11月28日**

## 1. 簡介
歡迎使用「Explore Diving 探索潛水」（以下簡稱「本應用程式」）。本應用程式由獨立開發者開發與維護。我們非常重視使用者的隱私權，本隱私權政策旨在說明我們如何處理您的資料。

**簡而言之**：本應用程式採「離線優先」設計，我們**不收集、不儲存、也不傳輸**您的任何個人識別資料（PII）至我們的伺服器。

## 2. 我們收集的資料

### 2.1 個人資料
我們不收集任何個人資料。本應用程式不需要註冊或登入。您的使用習慣、搜尋紀錄皆僅存在於您當下的裝置記憶體與儲存空間中。

### 2.2 位置資訊 (Location)
本應用程式不會在背景主動追蹤您的 GPS 位置資訊。
* **地圖顯示**：您的位置資訊僅在本地端運算，用於在地圖上顯示您與潛點的相對位置。
* **導航功能**：當您使用「導航」功能時，本應用程式會將目的地的經緯度傳送至您裝置上已安裝的外部地圖應用程式（如 Google Maps 或 Apple Maps）進行導航。此過程由您的裝置系統處理，本應用程式不會紀錄此行為。

## 3. 第三方服務
為了提供完整的功能，本應用程式整合了以下第三方服務。這些服務可能會依其自身的隱私權政策處理基礎網路傳輸資料（如 IP 位址）：

* **地圖圖資 (OpenStreetMap & CartoDB)**：
    本應用程式的地圖背景使用 OpenStreetMap 資料並由 CartoDB 渲染。當您的裝置下載地圖區塊（Tiles）時，相關請求（包含 IP 位址）將直接傳送至圖資伺服器以進行流量傳輸，這是網際網路運作的標準程序。
    * [OpenStreetMap 隱私權政策](https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy)
    * [Carto 隱私權政策](https://carto.com/privacy/)

* **外部導航**：
    點擊導航連結將跳轉至外部服務，其資料收集行為依循 Google 與 Apple 的隱私權政策。

## 4. 廣告
目前版本不包含任何第三方廣告軟體開發套件（Ad SDK），亦不會進行廣告追蹤。

## 5. 兒童隱私
本應用程式不針對 13 歲以下兒童行銷，亦不有意收集兒童的個人資料。

## 6. 政策更新
我們可能會不時更新本隱私權政策以符合法規要求或反應功能變更。更新後的政策將發布於此頁面。建議您定期查看以獲取最新資訊。

## 7. 聯絡我們
若您對本隱私權政策有任何疑問，請透過電子郵件與我們聯繫
